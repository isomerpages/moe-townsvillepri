### 2021

**SMOPS** <br>
SMOPS (Singapore Mathematical Olympiad for Primary Schools) is an annual event that sets out to generate greater interest in Mathematics among students in primary schools and sharpen their problem-solving skills. 

These students have taken part in SMOPS 2021 and won the following awards listed below. In recognition of their performance, Townsville Primary School has also received the Honorable Team Awards.
